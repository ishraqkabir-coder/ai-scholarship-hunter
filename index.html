About AI Scholarship Hunter

Innovation in progress! Built specifically for Frostbyte, this demo showcases the core potential of my AI Scholarship Hunter. I am committed to further refining every detail to ensure a polished, high-performance, and production-ready solution.

AI Scholarship Hunter: Bridging the Gap to Global Education

Finding the right scholarship is often the most overwhelming part of a student’s journey. Deadlines, eligibility criteria, and scattered information make it easy to miss life-changing opportunities. AI Scholarship Hunter was built to solve this—putting the power of real-time Artificial Intelligence directly into your hands.

The Technology Stack

Built with a privacy-first, client-side approach, this application runs entirely in your browser.

  • HTML5 & Tailwind CSS: For a modern, responsive, and “glass-morphic” interface that provides a premium user experience.
  • Vanilla JavaScript: No heavy frameworks—just pure, high-performance JS handling form logic, API calls, and local caching.
  • OpenRouter & Perplexity Sonar: The brain of the app. Unlike static AI models, it performs real-time web searches to ensure you get live, up-to-date scholarship data.
  • Privacy-Centric: No databases, no sign-ups. Your data stays in your browser’s localStorage.
Key Features
  • Personalized Match Scoring: The AI calculates a percentage score based on your GPA, IELTS, and extracurriculars, even explaining why a scholarship fits you.
  • Live Research Engine: Goes beyond simple lists. It digs into Reddit, blogs, and forums to find success stories of past recipients from your specific country.
  • Multimedia Integration: Automatically fetches relevant YouTube guides and vlogs to give you a visual roadmap for your application.

Developed for the Frostbyte Hackathon, this platform is more than just a search tool; it’s a personal consultant. While this is a functional demo, the roadmap includes even deeper integration with university portals and automated document checklists.

Welcome to AI Scholarship Hunter

Finding the right scholarship is one of the most overwhelming challenges a student can face. AI Scholarship Hunter puts the power of real-time AI directly into your hands.

Step 1 — Tell Us About You

Fill in the 3-step form with your academic background, citizenship, English scores, and preferences. Every field you fill in makes your results more accurate.

Step 2 — AI Searches in Real Time

Once you submit, Perplexity Sonar—a live web-search AI—scans the internet and returns 8 scholarships ranked by match score, tailored to your exact profile.

Step 3 — Review Your Results

Each result card shows the scholarship name, country, funding type, amount, deadline, and the reasons it matches you. Click Apply Now to visit the official page.

Step 4 — Deep Research

Click Deep Research on any card to open a full analysis in a new tab—covering requirements, documents, pros & cons, past recipient stories, and YouTube guides.
